Company Profile

AZUBROS COMMERCIAL ENTERPRISE started up about 3 decades ago, business concerned with the automotive industry which initially started off with trading of motorcycle auto spare parts in Kano Nigeria, then further evolved into retail sale of commercial motorcycles. With 17 years into the retail business, the company moved onto importation of it branded motorcycles named “KINGCHENG” from China. The company engages in sales of its branded motorcycles and motorcycles shoe brakes in other region of the country, she is also a major distributor of other imported automotive brands nmaely; Jincheng, suzuki, Honda, and Bajaj.
